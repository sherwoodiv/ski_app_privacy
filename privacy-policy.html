Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Boys Trip is a privacy-first app. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information to third parties.

Overview

Boys Trip ("the App") is a ski tracking app developed by Henry Sherwood. This policy describes what data the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Data We Collect

All data collected by the App is stored locally on your device and is never sent to external servers.

  • Profile Information — Your display name and optional profile photo, entered by you during setup.
  • Location Data — GPS coordinates, speed, and elevation while tracking is active. Used to calculate ski runs, vertical feet, speed, and distance.
  • Motion Data — Accelerometer readings used to detect falls and distinguish skiing from driving. This data is processed in real time and not stored persistently.
  • Session Data — Ski runs, lift rides, speed samples, challenge results, and session metadata. Stored in a local database on your device.

How We Use Your Data

  • Track your skiing activity (speed, vertical feet, runs, lift rides)
  • Detect falls via accelerometer data
  • Distinguish skiing from driving to let you trim your session
  • Display day recaps, leaderboards, and trip summaries
  • Sync stats with nearby friends over your local Wi-Fi network (see Group Sync below)

Group Sync (Local Network Only)

When you join a group session, the App shares your profile name, profile photo, and aggregated ski stats (runs, vertical, top speed, etc.) with other devices on the same local Wi-Fi network. This uses Apple's Bonjour protocol for peer-to-peer discovery.

  • Data is transmitted directly between devices on your local network.
  • No data passes through any external server.
  • Sync only occurs while you are in an active session with a group code.
  • Raw GPS coordinates are never shared — only derived statistics.

Camera and Photos

The App may request access to your camera and photo library for two purposes:

  • Setting a profile photo
  • Scanning a QR code to join a group

Photos are stored locally on your device and shared only via local network group sync (see above). The App does not upload photos to any server.

In-App Purchases

The App includes an optional tip jar powered by Apple's StoreKit. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. The App never accesses, stores, or transmits any payment or financial information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, or any other third party.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in a local SQLite database within the App's sandbox on your device.
  • Data is protected by iOS's built-in app sandboxing and device encryption.
  • Your data may be included in iOS device backups (iCloud or local) according to your device settings.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it.
  • You can delete individual sessions from within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require an account and does not transmit data to external services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
